About Us

Leptis was incorporated in 2016 based in UAE as a part of the business diversification of Abreco Freight, UAE offering Logistics services to their client. Within a short span of it's formation, the group was able to expand into multiple streams of services namely, Leptis Own Tranding LLP India, Leptis General Trading, Leptis Fresh, Leptis Exports, Al Ameen Hypermarket, Leptis Hypermarket, Leptis Shopping Center, Spice village Restaurant, Mecalo & Lassi Home. Leptis group has built a strong name by supplying top quality produce and offering a high level of customer satisfaction throughout it's products and services. In order to maintain customer satisfaction, Leptis is constantly focused on shopping trends and responds quickly and effectively to customer suggestions and feedback. Our in-store system is committed to ensuring a hygienic, controlled and safe environment. Leptis offers an ultra-modern shopping ambience by integrating all conceivable needs of the consumers under one roof and our different streams of businesses emphasizes uncompromisingly on convinience, accessibitlity, and providing top quality products and services at affordable prices to our beloved customers.
